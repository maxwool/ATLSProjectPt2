WHO WE ARE.

We are a grassroots movement working to build a fossil-free future powered by 100% renewable energy, and we empower communities across Colorado to join together to fight for
environmental justice. Through our volunteer-led teams, we use a variety of tactics, such as legislation and direct action, to take on the climate crisis.

Together we have the power to solve the climate crisis ‚Äì join over 20,000 Coloradans who have signed up to take action!

WHAT SETS US APART.

We are an independent state affiliate of the international environmental organization 350.org. Together, 350 is organizing around the world to end the use of fossil fuels and secure a just transition to renewable energy.
